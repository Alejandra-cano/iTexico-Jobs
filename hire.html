Here are some hints for the interview(s)

  • Be yourself, we really want to get to know you
  • Be honest and admit when you don't know the answer to a question. There's no harm on not knowing an answer to a given question, in fact, we really appreciate honesty.

FAQ

Will you let me know when a position has been filled?

We will send you an email to notify you if we already cover the vacancy. However, our recruiters might contact you, if we find a different potential match for your skills and experience.

Can I apply for more than one position at a time?

Yes, you can apply for more than one role at once, we recommend that you choose a few jobs with which your skills truly match.

How long should I wait to apply for a position again if I didn't get selected the first time?

We ask you  to consider  waiting 6 months to gain additional experience before reapplying
